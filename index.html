Why Classcord?

Easy

Classcord is designed to be as simple as possible for both people with experience in discord and people who cannot use it without a problem.

Verification system

Classcord incorporates a verification system with which to easily and securely assign student and teacher roles.

Reminders

Classcord incorporates a reminder system with which you can schedule tasks and review them at any time.

Events

Classcord incorporates a system of events with which teachers can assign tests, homework, readings... and students can consult them at any time.

Pomodoro

Classcord includes the famous concentration method called "pomodoro" to improve productivity in our tasks.

Study time tracking

Classcord includes a system for tacking study time to know how many hours we have been working.

Ask system

Classcord incorporates a simple command-based question system that students can use to ask their teachers questions in a quick and organized manner.

Best discord experience

Classcord includes a series of commands that allow a complete and easy experience of discord calls. With these commands the teacher can easily modify aspects such as the microphone of his students.
